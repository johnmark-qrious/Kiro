# Prayer Tracker App

**Name:** Prayer Tracker
**Tagline:** "See how God moves in my life"

## Concept
Evidence-based faith tracking. Log prayers, mark answered, see patterns over time. The app that proves God is active in your life.

## Key Differentiators (vs Echo Prayer, PrayerMate, Amen, etc.)
- **Ebenezer Timeline** - retrospective view showing God's pattern across months/years
- **Pattern Detection** - "70% of work prayers answered within 3 months"
- **Year-in-Review** - "2025: 47 prayers, 31 answered, here's the timeline"
- **Bible study + prayer in ONE place** (journal + tracker combined)
- **No gamification** - no streaks, no scores. Just honest tracking.
- **Simplicity** - no social feed, no devotional bloat, no subscription for basic features

## Competitors
- Echo Prayer (closest - tracks answered prayers, $3.99/mo premium)
- PrayerMate (free, organizes lists, no answered tracking)
- Amen (new 2026, person-centered cards, AI prompts, $14.99/yr)
- Pray.com / Hallow (content apps, not trackers)

## Gap in Market
Nobody does retrospective pattern visualization. Echo marks prayers answered but doesn't show the PATTERN or give the "zoom out" view.

## Pricing Norms
- Free tier mandatory
- Premium: $3-5/month or $15-30/year
- Recommended: Freemium. Free = log + mark answered. Paid = insights, timeline visualization, export.

## Tech (Recommended)
- Start as PWA (Next.js). Ship in 2-3 weeks. Validate.
- Go native later if retention proves out.

## Wargame Verdict
- Low risk, clear differentiation, you're the target user
- Kill threshold: 3 months, < 100 WAU
- Realistic ceiling: lifestyle business ($1-5K/month)

## Core UX Concept: Prayer as Story

Each prayer isn't a checkbox. It's a living story with chapters:
- **Start:** What you prayed and why
- **Updates:** Journal entries as things develop (fears, partial answers, changes)
- **Answer:** HOW it was answered (the full story, not just "yes/no")

Timeline view shows all prayers as rows (Gantt chart of faithfulness). Tap any one to read the full story. Zoom out = pattern. Zoom in = evidence.

The "How" field when marking answered is the killer feature. That's where the evidence lives. That's what you read back in 5 years and can't deny.
