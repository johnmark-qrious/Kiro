### 2026-06-04 - Foundation Session (No single passage - survey)

**Topics covered:**
- Textual transmission: 5,800+ Greek MSS, 30-year gap, 98%+ agreement
- Historical reliability: 8 criteria (early date, eyewitnesses, multiple sources, hostile corroboration, embarrassment, archaeology, no motive, external sources)
- Dead Sea Scrolls: Isaiah scroll 95%+ match over 1,000 years
- Problem passages: Mark 16:9-20, John 7:53-8:11, 1 John 5:7 (none doctrinal)
- Trinity: One being, three persons. Being = essence. Person = distinct center of consciousness/will.
- Creation/science: Genesis answers WHO/WHY, science answers HOW/WHEN. Different questions, not competing.
- Flood: Real catastrophic event(s). "Kol ha'erets" = all the known land, not necessarily globe-covering.
- Hearing God: Spectrum from audible (rare) to inner conviction (normal). David used Urim/Thummim via priest. New Covenant = Spirit indwelling (better access, less mechanical).
- Knowing Jesus: Power of his resurrection = death-to-life pattern operating in your circumstances.

**Key passages referenced:**
- 1 Corinthians 15:3-7 (earliest creed, 2-5 years after events)
- 1 Corinthians 15:14 ("if Christ has not been raised, faith is futile" - falsifiability)
- Philippians 3:10 (knowing Christ - power of resurrection + sharing in sufferings)
- Hebrews 4:12 (word is living and active)
- John 3:8 (wind/Spirit analogy)
- Hebrews 1:1-2 (God spoke through prophets, now through Son)
- John 14:23 (obey → closeness follows)
- Matthew 6:9-13 (Lord's Prayer - template for what to pray)
- James 1:5 (ask for wisdom - guaranteed answer)

**Connections discovered:**
- David needing a priest to consult God via ephod = delegating to sub-agents. New Covenant = Spirit internalized (always-on, no-latency, no-token-cost version).
- Bible "secrets" = developer easter eggs. Planted by authors who didn't know the full codebase. Only visible when you've seen enough of the system.
- The Bible's pattern (creation → fall → exile → redemption → restoration) maps to every individual human life.

**Questions raised:**
- How to distinguish "God answered" from "natural process" → Answer: false binary. God works through natural processes. Track patterns over years.
- Why miracles less common now than in Acts? → Miracles cluster at redemptive-historical breakthroughs + where natural means aren't available. Paul also had unanswered prayer (thorn) and unhealed friends (Trophimus).

### 2026-06-15 - What Is Faith? (Topical study)

**Topics covered:**
- Faith defined: pistis = knowledge (notitia) + assent (assensus) + trust (fiducia)
- Hebrews 11:1 — confidence in God's promises, not blind belief
- Faith comes by hearing God's word (Romans 10:17) — it's a response, not self-generated
- Hebrews 11 pattern: God spoke → they believed → they obeyed. Every time.
- Hebrews 11:35-39 — people of commended faith who did NOT receive what was promised
- Faith's object is God himself, not faith in faith (the power is in the One believed, not the believing)
- Disciples failing to cast out demon (Mark 9/Matt 17): operated on autopilot, disconnected from source
- Two-realm framework: biblical (Eph 6:12, Eph 2:6) but faith isn't a force you project — it's alignment with God's will flowing from heaven to earth
- Healing in the atonement: purchase is complete, delivery timing is God's sovereign choice (Paul's thorn, Trophimus left sick)
- How God communicated pre-Bible: direct speech, dreams/visions, creation, conscience, priests/prophets, theophanies
- God actively pursues being known (Jer 29:13, James 4:8, Rev 3:20)
- Genuine seeking always finds — the promise is unconditional (Cornelius pattern in Acts 10)

**Key passages referenced:**
- Hebrews 11:1 (faith defined — assurance of things hoped for)
- Hebrews 11:35-39 (faith commended WITHOUT receiving the promise)
- Romans 10:17 (faith comes by hearing)
- Mark 9:14-29 / Matthew 17:14-21 (disciples fail — "this kind only by prayer")
- Isaiah 53:5, 1 Peter 2:24 (healing in atonement)
- 2 Corinthians 12:7-9 (Paul's thorn — God said no)
- Romans 1:19-20 (God knowable through creation)
- Romans 2:14-15 (law written on hearts / conscience)
- Acts 10 (Cornelius — God sends message to genuine seeker)
- Jeremiah 29:13 (seek with whole heart → find)
- James 4:8 (draw near → He draws near)
- Exodus 33:11 (God spoke to Moses face to face)
- John 7:17 (willingness to obey = condition for knowing)

**Key takeaways:**
1. Faith is trust in a Person, not a force you generate
2. Faith is a response to God speaking, not human initiation
3. Faith is not measured by outcomes (Heb 11:39)
4. Faith is active connection, not past confidence
5. Faith and submission coexist (bold asking + "not my will")
6. God takes responsibility for being findable — always has
7. Genuine seeking always finds

**Things unlearned:**
- Faith is NOT a spiritual force/substance you project to "activate" heavenly resources
- Faith does NOT obligate God to deliver on your timeline
- Lack of healing does NOT equal lack of faith (Paul disproves this)
- Faith is NOT the absence of doubt (Mark 9:24)

**Questions raised:**
- How does faith practically grow? (Romans 10:17 cycle — drill next time)
- What's the difference between faith and presumption? (boundary worth exploring)

### 2026-06-04 - What to Pray For (Template)

**The Lord's Prayer categories (Matthew 6:9-13):**
1. His glory (hallowed be your name)
2. His kingdom advancing (your kingdom come)
3. His will over mine (your will be done)
4. Daily provision (daily bread - today, not stockpiling)
5. Confession + releasing others (forgive as we forgive)
6. Protection (lead not into temptation, deliver from evil)

**Order matters:** God's concerns first (1-3), then yours (4-6).

**Guaranteed answer:** Wisdom (James 1:5). Ask daily.

**Daily minimum prayer:**
> "Father - show me one thing today I need to see. Give me wisdom for the decisions in front of me. Forgive where I've drifted. Protect me from the stuff I can't see coming. And make me useful to someone else today."

### 2026-06-04 - Christianity's Unique Position

The only religion that says "disprove this ONE event (resurrection) and throw the whole thing out" (1 Cor 15:14). Every other religion rests on private revelation or unfalsifiable experience. Christianity stakes everything on a public, investigable, historical claim.

### 2026-06-04 - The Silence Isn't Rejection

Feeling God's absence ≠ God being absent. David, Paul, and Jesus all experienced silence. The response: keep showing up. Write instead of speak. Pray Scripture back. Shorten it. Walk and talk. 2 honest minutes beats 20 minutes of duty.
